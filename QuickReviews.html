Either / Or, Elliott Smith

Song/Ranking Thoughts
  1. Alameda - 10/10
  2. Between the Bars - 10/10
  3. Say Yes - 10/10
  4. Rose Parade - 10/10
  5. Angeles - 10/10
  6. Speed Trials - 10/10
  7. 2:45 AM - 10/10
  8. Punch and Judy - 10/10
  9. Ballad of Big Nothing - 10/10
  10. Pictures of Me - 9.7/10
  11. No Name No. 5 - 9.3/10
  12. Cupid's Trick - 9/10
 This album is as near to perfect as any has ever come. Elliott was a genius; an unrivaled songwriter, an unrecognized guitar virtuoso, and a master of honest musical-emotional expression. Though it may appear, upon first listen, an unremarkable lo-fi indie record, it's magic pulls you in slowly over multiple listens, and once it clicks with you, you'll adore it forever.

Score: 10/10
